Bonhomme was asked to redesign Total Group's news website: Politiques énergétiques.

More than just a content platform, Politiques énergétiques is thought of as a media of its own, which serves, both as a forum for industry leaders and a discussion space for all.

Skills

  • Art Direction
  • Project Management
  • Front-end Development
  • Back-end Development

Branded content

Creating a full visual identity.
A range of pictograms and colors to organize the content.

Intuitive exploration

The side panels and the geographic search feature allows for cross navigation.

Invitation to debate

A hashtag allows users to react in real time during video debates.

Developer Focus: Integration of the Youtube Live Embedding feature with the Twitter API to offer a live participative debate.
