To round off 2014, Arte Concert wanted to offer its users a selection of highlights from the past year.
Bonhomme designed "#Gifmylive, 1 year of live concerts 1 year of emotion", an interactive social experience bringing together all the emotions and excitement of Arte Concert's live shows.

A new way of discovering music

Curiosity is sparked as users navigate the site using emotions, through nine different pages, each one graphically representing a different state of mind.

Immersive and interactive pages

Each page combines interactive suprematist forms, animated GIFs and sound loops.

Developer Focus: HTML5 canvas elements associated to a Javascript physics engine to give harmonious interactions.

A viral and participatory platform

By sharing their favorite live concert of the year, internet users defined the key emotion of 2014.
