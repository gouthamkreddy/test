Client : Double 2 x Audi

Date : October 2014

For Double 2 agency Bonhomme creates the mobile app "Audi Spotted". Designed for the community of fans of the brand, the application allows them to apply filters to photos of their favorite Audi models.

Skills

  • Art Direction
  • Project Management

Challenging users

A personalized dashboard invites the user
to take up challenges to unlock new filters.

Bringing together a community of fans

Brand enthusiats come together on a social wall where they can discover
the pictures taken by members of the community and share their own.

Viral brand culture

A range of filters created to fit
with the brand's world gives a sense of visual unity
to its fans' photographs.
